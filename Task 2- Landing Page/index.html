Course We Offer

We offer course by Eduford

Intermediate

The Intermediate Courses are taught at the Government or Private or Cooperative Junior Colleges for two years. These Courses are followed by prestigious State level Entrance Tests for admission to the various Degree Colleges offering Professional or general education.

Degree

Typically, Degree courses are taught through a combination of lectures, tutorials, seminars, computer practice sessions, extensive practical laboratory work and project work (individual and group). Some institutions will offer online teaching, as well as site visits (or field work).

Post-graduation

We offer you a foundational understanding of the domain of your choice with varied elective courses, opportunities for independent study and guest lectures, workshops and conferences. Our faculty have extensive experience in teaching, research and field practice.
